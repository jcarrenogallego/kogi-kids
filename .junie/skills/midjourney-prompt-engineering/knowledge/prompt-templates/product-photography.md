# Product Photography Templates

Prompts for commercial product shots, e-commerce, and advertising imagery.

## Core Principles
- Clear subject specification
- Defined surface/setting
- Specific lighting style
- Clean background treatment
- Commercial quality indicators

**Universal parameters:** `--ar [ratio] --s 100-150 --style raw`

---

## Classic Studio Shots

### Clean White Background
```
[Product] centered on white surface, soft studio lighting, clean white background, subtle shadow beneath product, professional product photography, commercial quality --ar 4:5 --s 100 --style raw
```

### Dramatic Dark Background
```
[Product] isolated on pure black background, dramatic studio lighting, strong rim light highlighting edges, professional product photography, high contrast --ar 4:5 --s 120 --style raw
```

## Lighting Setups

| Setup | Prompt Fragment |
|-------|-----------------|
| Soft Box | soft diffused studio lighting, even illumination, minimal shadows |
| Rim Light | dramatic rim lighting highlighting edges, dark background, strong contrast |
| Natural Window | soft natural window light from side, warm atmosphere, lifestyle feel |
| Hard Light | directional hard lighting creating defined shadows, texture emphasis |
| Backlit | backlit creating glow around edges, soft front fill, ethereal |
| Multi-Point | key light from 45 degrees, fill light opposite, subtle rim light |

## Surface/Background Options

**Solid:** pure white, light grey seamless, deep black, [color] gradient
**Natural:** raw wood, white marble, concrete, natural linen, slate stone
**Lifestyle:** minimalist desk, kitchen counter, rustic wooden table

## Negative Prompts for Product Photography
```
--no text, watermark, logo, label (unless you want them)
--no people, hands (unless intentional)
--no busy background, clutter
--no distorted proportions
```
